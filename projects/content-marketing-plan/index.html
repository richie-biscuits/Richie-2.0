Living document for the Polynize 30 Days series and ongoing social content strategy

Series Overview

30 Days with OpenCore — Marrs documents the journey of building with OpenClaw, sharing real insights, real builds, and real results. Each video showcases one specific thing built and one key insight.

Content Pillars

01 — The Hidden Cost of Drowning

Named, specific pain points SME founders live through. Drowning in ops work. Missing opportunities. Losing to bigger competitors.

02 — Named Agents, Named Jobs

"Meet Tabatha, the deal flow agent we built for X." First-person agent introductions with personalities and specific capabilities.

03 — The Hiring Maths

Cost comparisons that reframe capacity. "The sales rep you were going to hire for $90K... vs. the agent team that costs $2,500."

04 — Humans, Elevated

Stories where the human is the hero. A founder reclaiming their week. A department lead who stopped being the bottleneck.

05 — Behind the Build

How we think. The concept of drift. Why configuration beats tooling. Short explainer content that positions us as builders with a point of view.

06 — The Polynize Perspective

Our angle on the wider AI-and-work conversation. The cognitive unit. The real bottleneck. Thought leadership without the thought-leader voice.

Voice & Language

Always Use

  • Agent (not bot, not assistant)
  • Agentic Team
  • Install (not configure, program)
  • Deploy (not launch, release)
  • Service fee (not subscription)
  • Expert thinking
  • Trained on your business
  • Blueprint
  • Human lead
  • Drift
  • Specific numbers, named tools
  • Named agents with personalities

Never Use

  • Unlock, leverage, empower, game-changer
  • Delve, cutting-edge, robust, seamless
  • Chatbot, AI assistant (generic)
  • Replace, fire, eliminate headcount
  • Hosting, maintenance, subscription
  • Solution, platform, ecosystem, synergy
  • Powered by AI, AI-driven
  • "Imagine if..." / "What if..."
  • Em dashes (use commas or colons)
  • Vague claims without numbers
  • Anything implying humans replaced
