# Mission Control Builder — Project Plan

## Goal
Build a self-serve web app where businesses and individuals can design, deploy, and manage their own agent teams with Polynize infrastructure.

## Purpose
Two birds, one stone:
1. **Internal tool** — build MC for ourselves with Kenny
2. **Product** — public SaaS product that generates leads and revenue

The public product becomes the call-to-action on Polynize social posts.

## Target Users
- Melbourne founders/SMEs who want AI agents but can't build them
- Businesses who want a visual dashboard for their agent team
- Agent builders who want a project management layer for AI
