## Discovery & Visual Communication

### Excalidraw + Claude Integration
**What it does:** Real-time collaborative whiteboard with AI-powered diagram generation. In discovery meetings, as clients describe their business, workflows, and pain points — you can generate diagrams on the fly using AI.

**How Polynize uses it:** During discovery calls, use Excalidraw to collaboratively sketch out the client's current process, then use Claude/AI to generate architecture diagrams, flowcharts, and system maps in real-time. Turns vague descriptions into visual clarity instantly.

**Why it's powerful for sales:** Clients see their own business mapped out in front of them. Makes abstract AI conversations concrete. Very "wow factor" in demos.

**Pricing:** Free tier available. Pro from ~$12/user/month.

**Integration:** Works in browser. AI diagram generation via Anthropic Claude or built-in AI features. Export to PNG, SVG, PDF.

**URL:** https://excalidraw.com

**Category:** discovery, visual, diagrams, client communication
