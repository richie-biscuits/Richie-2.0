## Feeding Schedule

Cats are fed **twice a day**: morning around 7:00am and evening around 5:00pm. Being off by an hour is fine.

### Massa & Viva
- Each meal: **half a can each** of the *Kidney Care Chicken and Vegetable Stew* (the small cans)
- If they leave food or seem uninterested, try offering the **Hills Prescription Diet Kidney Care Pâté with Tuna** — scoop a small amount into one of the little bowls (in the second drawer next to the stove) and place it near their regular bowl
- If they still don't eat, it's probably just an off meal — that's okay
- **Toss any leftover wet food** — don't leave it sitting

### Merlin
- Each meal: **half a can** of *Royal Canin Gastrointestinal Kitten* (one full can = one full day)
- **Plus:** top up his dry *Maine Coon Kitten* biscuits — make sure they're topped up before bed as he snacks overnight
- **Evening only:** mix in **one sachet of Purina ProPlan Feline ProBiotics** into his wet food
- If he's not eating his wet food, he's probably just slobbered on it too much — toss it and give fresh biscuits instead
- Merlin gets wet food + biscuits in the morning, and biscuits topped up throughout the day as needed

## What to Watch For

**Pees or poops outside the litter box** — this can happen when cats are stressed. Check the following:
- **Always check the top of the bed before getting in.** Massa has been known to poo on the bed.
- The blankets on top of the bed are **waterproof**, so if there's a pee it won't go through.

**If there's an accident on the bed:**
1. Mop it up with paper towel
2. Put the whole blanket in the washing machine:
   - Quick 30 cycle
   - Press Intensive
   - Max spin rate
   - Cold wash
   - Detergent in sections 1 and 2
3. Spare sheets and blankets are in the **last door of the cupboards in the bedroom**
4. Cleaning kit is in the hallway — it'll be left somewhere visible

**If a cat seems unwell** — call Abby the Vet (0401 682 015) or the vet assistant (0416 905 810) straight away.
